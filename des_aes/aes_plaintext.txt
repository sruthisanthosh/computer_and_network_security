• AES stands for Advanced Encryption Standard
• Rijndael cipher was selected as the AES in Oct-2000
• Designed by Vincent Rijmen and Joan Daemen in Belgium
• Symmetric Bock cipher
• Adapted by National Institute of Standards and Technology in 2001
• Widely used due to its simplicity and increased security
• Convert 128-bit plaintext to 128-bit ciphertext
• Resistant against Brute-force and Statistical attacks
