• DES is a symmetric-key block cipher.
• Use Feistel Cipher structure
• Adopted by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
in 1977.
• Replaced by Advanced Encryption Standard (AES).
• Input : 64 bits
• Output : 64 bits
• Main key : 64 bits
• Subkey : 56 bits
• Round key : 48 bits
• No. of rounds : 16 rounds
